\chapter{Execution Videos}
\label{app:execution_videos}

This appendix provides links to supplementary videos illustrating selected executions of the
multi-robot coordination system.

\section{Large-Scale Heuristic Execution}
\label{app:large_scale_heuristics_video}

The video available at \url{https://youtu.be/P9jD-t3u8gc} is associated with the large-scale factory
operation scenarios presented in Chapter~\ref{chap:ch6}, specifically the three
180 robot instances described in Section~\ref{subsec:large_scale_factory_operation_scenarios}. It
shows the planned robot movements for the "docking to warehouse", "warehouse to warehouse" and
"warehouse to docking" configurations.

\section{Batching Mechanism Execution}
\label{app:batching_video}

The video available at \url{https://youtu.be/IMvVRX2669w} shows the integrated coordination
system running without batching and with batching. It is included as an illustrative complement to
the batching mechanism evaluation presented in Chapter~\ref{chap:temporal_consistency_batching}.
