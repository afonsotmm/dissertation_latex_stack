\chapter{Background and Fundamental Aspects} \label{chap:ch2}
\paragraph{}

This chapter presents the fundamental concepts and background knowledge required to contextualize
the research developed in this dissertation. Section~\ref{sec:problem-definition} and Section~\ref{sec:mapf-representation} introduces a
formal definition of the MAPF problem and its representation, respectively. Section~\ref{sec:single-agent-search} explore classical graph search algorithms that serve as building blocks for many MAPF methods. Finally,
Section~\ref{sec:parallel-concurrent} introduces foundational notions of parallel and concurrent
computing.
