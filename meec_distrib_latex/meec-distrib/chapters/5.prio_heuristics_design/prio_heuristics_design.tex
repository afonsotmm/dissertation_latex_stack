\chapter{Prioritization Heuristics Design and Development}
\label{chap:ch5}
\hspace{\parindent}
After stabilizing the baseline coordination pipeline, the work focused on improving the TEA* planner through priority ordering. Since TEA* plans robots sequentially, each planned path constrains the following ones, making the chosen ordering directly affect solution cost, delays, and feasibility.

This chapter presents the prioritization heuristics developed to generate alternative robot orderings for TEA* and support the parallel exploration framework introduced in the next chapter.
